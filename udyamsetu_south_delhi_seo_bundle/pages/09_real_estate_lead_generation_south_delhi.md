## Section-by-section page structure and detailed copy

### 1. Hero section

In South Delhi real estate, a high lead count means little if the buyer has the wrong budget, wrong location, no timeline, or no site-visit intent. UdyamSetu builds property lead systems that combine landing pages, local SEO, ad experiments, WhatsApp qualification, and CRM-lite follow-up.

Hero copy: **Generate real estate enquiries that your sales team can actually work with.** Capture budget, location, property type, possession timeline, and visit readiness before the lead disappears.

CTA: **Get a Property Lead Funnel Audit.**

### 2. Real estate lead pain

South Delhi property buyers compare fast and ask specific questions. They want area, ticket size, layout, ownership status, parking, floor, nearby markets, and viewing availability. Brokers and builders often lose time with casual enquiries because the landing page and WhatsApp flow do not qualify early.

Use copy such as: **The goal is not more property chats. The goal is a cleaner pipeline of buyers, investors, and site-visit prospects.**

### 3. Property funnel components

Recommended deliverables:

- Project or property-category landing pages.
- Local area pages where relevant: GK, South Extension, Defence Colony, Vasant Vihar, Saket, Hauz Khas, Green Park.
- Budget and property-type qualification on WhatsApp.
- Site-visit call-to-action and reminder flow.
- CRM-lite pipeline: new, qualified, site visit scheduled, visited, negotiation, cold, lost.
- Retargeting page and ad experiment support.
- Weekly CPL and lead-quality report.

CTA: **Ask for a sample real estate qualification flow.**

### 4. Lead qualification questions

The page should show exactly what makes a real estate lead useful:

- Are they looking to buy, rent, sell, or invest?
- Preferred South Delhi locality?
- Budget range?
- Property type: builder floor, apartment, commercial, plot, office, showroom?
- Timeline: immediate, 30 days, 3 months, exploratory?
- Decision-maker status?
- Site-visit availability?

This builds credibility because it shows UdyamSetu understands property lead economics.

### 5. SEO + ads + WhatsApp strategy

A strong property funnel may need both organic and paid channels. SEO pages can target service-area and property category intent. Ads can test micro-markets or project offers faster. WhatsApp captures and qualifies the enquiry. Tracking shows which locality, property category, or creative is producing serious leads.

This page should avoid claiming guaranteed property sales. Position UdyamSetu as improving discovery, enquiry capture, qualification, and follow-up.

### 6. Process section

1. **Inventory and offer intake:** property categories, areas, ticket size, proof, photos, and sales process.
2. **Funnel map:** landing pages, ad tests, SEO pages, WhatsApp questions, and CRM stages.
3. **Build:** pages, CTAs, scripts, tracking, and reporting sheet.
4. **Launch:** run SEO and/or ad experiments.
5. **Lead review:** identify which leads are serious and which questions improve qualification.
6. **Optimize:** update landing pages, budget filters, visuals, and follow-up timing.

CTA: **WhatsApp 'PROPERTY' for a funnel review.**

### 7. Final CTA

Close with: **Stop guessing which property leads are worth calling. Build a funnel that qualifies before your team spends time.** Button: **Get My Property Lead Funnel Audit.**
