## Section-by-section page structure and detailed copy

### 1. Hero section

Clinic enquiries often arrive when the front desk is busy with patients, calls, walk-ins, billing, or doctor coordination. UdyamSetu builds WhatsApp-first lead automation so appointment requests are acknowledged, qualified, routed, and reminded without depending entirely on manual follow-up.

Hero copy: **Help your South Delhi clinic capture appointment enquiries before patients move to the next search result.** Build WhatsApp flows, local SEO pages, appointment CTAs, and reminder systems around your real clinic workflow.

CTA: **Get a Clinic Lead Flow Audit.**

### 2. Clinic-specific pain section

A patient searching from Saket, Malviya Nagar, or Hauz Khas usually wants quick clarity: which service, which doctor, appointment availability, fee range if shared, location, and how to book. If the response is delayed, the patient may call another clinic.

This section should highlight practical issues: missed after-hours enquiries, repeated questions, unclear service pages, no reminder system, no lead status, and no connection between local SEO pages and front-desk workflow.

### 3. What the clinic automation system includes

Recommended deliverables:

- Service pages for key treatments or appointment types.
- Local SEO structure for South Delhi service areas.
- Click-to-WhatsApp appointment CTAs.
- Initial qualification flow: service needed, preferred timing, location, new/existing patient, urgency.
- Staff routing rules and backup notification.
- Appointment confirmation and reminder templates.
- CRM-lite lead sheet with status: new, contacted, booked, visited, follow-up.
- Weekly clinic report: enquiries, booked appointments, missed/pending, best pages.

CTA: **Ask for a sample clinic WhatsApp flow.**

### 4. Safety and compliance positioning

The page should be careful: WhatsApp automation should not provide medical diagnosis, emergency advice, or treatment guarantees. It should collect appointment intent, share approved clinic information, route patients to staff, and escalate urgent or sensitive messages to a human.

Suggested trust box: **Automation handles booking support. Medical decisions remain with qualified professionals.**

### 5. Clinic SEO + automation approach

A clinic page should combine local SEO with operational follow-through. If a patient lands on a dermatology, dental, physiotherapy, diagnostics, or consultation page, the CTA should not be a generic form. It should ask the right questions and help staff book faster. UdyamSetu can build pages that answer patient questions, add trust proof, and route enquiries through WhatsApp.

### 6. Process section

1. **Clinic intake:** services, doctors, hours, locations, FAQs, appointment rules, and staff handoffs.
2. **Page and CTA map:** decide service pages and appointment prompts.
3. **WhatsApp flow:** create approved messages, qualification questions, and escalation logic.
4. **Tracking:** record source page, service, status, and booking outcome.
5. **Launch:** test during clinic hours and after-hours.
6. **Improve:** reduce missed leads and refine reminders based on staff feedback.

CTA: **WhatsApp 'CLINIC' for a lead-flow review.**

### 7. Final CTA

Close with: **Let your front desk focus on patients, not repetitive enquiry sorting.** Button: **Get My Clinic Lead Flow Audit.** Supporting text: **Built for clinics across Saket, Malviya Nagar, Hauz Khas, GK, Green Park, Defence Colony, and Lajpat Nagar.**
