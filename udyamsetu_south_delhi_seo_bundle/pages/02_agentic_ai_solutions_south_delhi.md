## Section-by-section page structure and detailed copy

### 1. Hero section

Some workflows are too complex for a simple chatbot. They require an AI system that can understand a goal, check context, use tools, draft the next action, and know when to ask a human. That is where agentic AI fits.

Suggested hero copy: **Build bounded AI agents that qualify leads, update CRM, draft responses, prepare reports, and move work forward—without removing human control.** UdyamSetu designs agentic AI workflows for South Delhi businesses that want faster execution but cannot risk messy automation.

CTA: **Check if your workflow is agent-ready.**

### 2. What makes this different from a chatbot

A chatbot usually answers questions. An agentic workflow can complete a multi-step task. For example, when a new lead arrives, an AI agent can read the message, identify service intent, ask missing qualification questions, update a sheet or CRM, route the enquiry to the correct person, draft a follow-up, and create a reporting note.

The page should clarify that UdyamSetu does not recommend open-ended autonomy for sensitive decisions. The stronger conversion message is **bounded agents with rules, approvals, logs, and escalation.**

### 3. Agentic AI use cases for South Delhi businesses

Use cases should be local and concrete:

- **Clinic agent:** triage appointment requests, collect patient intent, suggest department routing, and send reminders after staff approval.
- **Real estate agent:** ask budget, property type, location preference, site-visit timing, and route hot leads to the sales owner.
- **B2B supplier agent:** capture product requirements, quantity, delivery location, urgency, and draft a quote request summary.
- **SEO/content agent:** monitor target pages, suggest internal links, draft updates, and prepare a weekly search visibility brief.
- **Owner dashboard agent:** compile leads, best-performing pages, pending follow-ups, and campaign notes into a WhatsApp-friendly report.

### 4. Agent design principles

Each agent needs a clear goal, allowed tools, data access, refusal rules, human approval gates, and error handling. UdyamSetu should frame its service as agent design plus business implementation.

Suggested copy: **We do not start by asking which model to use. We start by defining what the agent is allowed to do.** A sales qualification agent should not invent pricing. A clinic support agent should not give medical advice. A real estate agent should not promise availability without a verified source. These rules make the system useful and safer.

### 5. Implementation workflow

1. **Agent opportunity audit:** identify workflows with repeated decisions and clear outcomes.
2. **Task decomposition:** split the workflow into trigger, context, reasoning, action, handoff, and reporting.
3. **Knowledge base setup:** add FAQs, pricing rules, service areas, scripts, project data, or lead criteria.
4. **Tool connection:** connect WhatsApp, website forms, Sheets, CRM, calendar, email, or reporting dashboards where appropriate.
5. **Pilot and logs:** test with historical leads, then launch in a narrow scope.
6. **Optimization:** review failures, refine prompts, add guardrails, and expand only where performance is reliable.

CTA: **Request an AI Agent Feasibility Call.**

### 6. Trust/proof section copy

Add proof in the form of workflow diagrams, sample agent logs, before/after response time, lead routing examples, and a list of non-negotiable safeguards. Trust language: **Your team remains the decision-maker. The agent handles repeatable steps, prepares context, and reduces manual follow-up load.**

### 7. Final CTA section

Close with: **Not every workflow needs an agent. The first call tells you whether agentic AI, simple automation, a better website form, or SEO cleanup will create the highest ROI.** Button: **WhatsApp 'AGENT' for a feasibility check.**

## FAQ section

### What is agentic AI in simple business terms?
Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can work through multi-step tasks, use tools, and take bounded actions toward a goal, while following rules and escalation paths.

### How is this different from AI automation?
AI automation may follow fixed rules. Agentic AI is useful when the workflow requires context, decision steps, drafting, routing, or tool use across multiple systems.

### Can AI agents work with WhatsApp leads?
Yes, an agent can help qualify WhatsApp leads, ask missing questions, summarize intent, route the enquiry, and prepare follow-up drafts when designed with clear guardrails.

### Do agentic AI systems need human approval?
For business-critical, medical, legal, pricing, or commitment-related tasks, human approval should be part of the workflow. UdyamSetu should position this as a safeguard.

### Which South Delhi businesses are good candidates?
Clinics, real estate teams, B2B suppliers, consultants, education businesses, and service companies with repeated enquiries and structured follow-up rules are strong candidates.
