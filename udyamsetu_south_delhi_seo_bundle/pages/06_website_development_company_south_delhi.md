## Section-by-section page structure and detailed copy

### 1. Hero section

A website should not only look modern. It should explain your offer, handle local search intent, capture enquiries, and help your team respond quickly. UdyamSetu builds fast, mobile-first websites and landing-page systems for South Delhi businesses that want measurable enquiries.

Hero copy: **Build a website that works like a lead system—not a digital brochure.** Get service pages, location pages, WhatsApp CTAs, tracking, SEO-ready structure, and conversion copy in one build.

CTA: **Request a Website Conversion Audit.**

### 2. Why many business websites fail

Many websites have attractive banners but weak service clarity. They do not answer pricing or process questions, they hide the CTA, they load slowly on mobile, they lack local pages, and they do not track which page generated a lead. In South Delhi, where buyers compare quickly, a vague website sends serious enquiries to competitors.

Suggested line: **Your website should make the next step obvious within seconds.**

### 3. What UdyamSetu builds

Core deliverables:

- Main website with clear service architecture.
- Conversion landing pages for campaigns and local SEO.
- Service-location pages where they have unique value.
- Mobile-first layout and lightweight page structure.
- Click-to-call and WhatsApp CTA placement.
- Offer block, proof block, FAQ block, and trust content.
- Tracking setup for GA4, Search Console, Pixel, and lead sheets where relevant.
- Schema recommendations for services, FAQs, breadcrumbs, and organization.

CTA: **Ask for a page map before redesigning.**

### 4. Website types by South Delhi audience

Make the page feel tailored:

- **Clinics:** appointment-focused pages, condition/service sections, trust and safety copy, WhatsApp booking prompts.
- **Real estate:** project pages, location proof, buyer qualification, site-visit CTA, inventory-update workflow.
- **B2B suppliers:** product category pages, quote forms, MOQ details, delivery areas, RFQ capture.
- **Professional services:** service clarity, credentials, process, consultation CTAs, case examples.
- **Institutes and local services:** program pages, parent/customer FAQs, testimonials, location relevance.

### 5. Build process

1. **Business intake:** services, locations, offers, proof, competitors, FAQs.
2. **Page map:** decide main pages, landing pages, local pages, and internal links.
3. **Copy and wireframe:** write sections around buyer questions and conversion logic.
4. **Design/build:** mobile-first layout, speed-conscious components, CTA hierarchy.
5. **Tracking and QA:** test forms, WhatsApp links, events, metadata, schema, and responsiveness.
6. **Launch and improve:** review clicks, leads, and page performance.

CTA: **WhatsApp 'WEBSITE' for a page map recommendation.**

### 6. Trust/proof section copy

Use portfolio cards and before/after examples. Highlight that UdyamSetu already builds local SEO pages, conversion landing pages, WhatsApp lead capture, and mobile-first pages. Add proof blocks for industries served: healthcare, logistics, education, travel, mobility, SaaS, local services, and B2B. Include a note: **No bloated template website. No copy-paste local pages. No CTA hidden in the footer.**

### 7. Final CTA

Close with a specific offer: **Send your current website. We will tell you the first 5 changes that can improve enquiries.** Button: **Get My Website Audit on WhatsApp.**
