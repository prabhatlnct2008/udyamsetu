### 1. Hero section

Nehru Place and Okhla businesses often sell practical things: IT services, hardware, software support, equipment, components, manufacturing services, distribution, repairs, and B2B supply. Their websites need to capture specific product or service intent, not just say 'contact us'.

Hero copy: **Build a B2B growth system for quote requests, product enquiries, service leads, and repeat follow-ups.** UdyamSetu combines SEO pages, website improvements, WhatsApp qualification, CRM-lite tracking, and AI automation for South Delhi business hubs.

### 2. Why B2B local SEO is different

A Nehru Place IT support company may need pages for laptop repair, networking, software, AMC, or cybersecurity support. An Okhla supplier may need product category pages, MOQ details, industrial use cases, export/distribution pages, and RFQ forms. The searcher is often comparing vendors and wants clarity fast.

Suggested copy: **B2B leads improve when your website asks the right questions before your team starts calling.**

### 3. Growth system components

Recommended components:

- Product/service-category landing pages.
- Local SEO pages for Nehru Place, Okhla, and relevant South Delhi service areas.
- RFQ or quote request form with product, quantity, urgency, delivery area, and business type.
- WhatsApp qualification flow for missing details.
- CRM-lite pipeline for enquiries, quotes sent, follow-up, won/lost, repeat orders.
- AI-assisted quote summary and follow-up drafting.
- Weekly report showing best pages, enquiry categories, and follow-up gaps.

CTA: **Ask for your RFQ page map.**

### 4. B2B automation use cases

Use cases should be specific:

- Incoming WhatsApp enquiry becomes a structured RFQ summary.
- Product catalogue questions are routed by category.
- Enquiries without quantity or delivery location are automatically asked for missing details.
- Follow-up reminders trigger after a quote is sent.
- Repeat enquiry data shows which product pages deserve SEO updates.
- Sales owner receives a concise summary instead of a long chat thread.

This section makes the page feel different from a generic SEO or website page.

### 5. SEO strategy for IT and industrial businesses

The page should recommend content based on buying intent: product category, service type, use case, industry served, locality, warranty/support, documents, FAQs, and comparison questions. Avoid thin locality-only pages. A page for 'networking AMC in Nehru Place' should differ from 'industrial equipment supplier Okhla' in proof, FAQs, CTA, and qualification fields.

### 6. Process section

1. **Business and catalogue intake:** products, services, industries, service areas, proof, pricing rules, and sales process.
2. **Keyword and page map:** product/service pages, local pages, and RFQ pages.
3. **Website and CTA build:** forms, WhatsApp prompts, proof blocks, FAQs, and schema.
4. **Automation setup:** route leads by product/service category and missing information.
5. **Reporting:** track enquiry type, source, quote status, and follow-up outcome.
6. **SEO improvement:** expand pages based on real enquiries and search data.

CTA: **WhatsApp 'B2B' for a growth-system audit.**

### 7. Final CTA

Close with: **Turn product and service enquiries into structured RFQs your team can act on.** Button: **Get My B2B Growth System Audit.** Supporting line: **Built for Nehru Place IT businesses, Okhla manufacturers and suppliers, distributors, and B2B service companies across South Delhi.**
