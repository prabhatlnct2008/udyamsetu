## Section-by-section page structure and detailed copy

### 1. Hero section

South Delhi businesses lose time and revenue when every enquiry, reminder, report, and follow-up depends on manual effort. UdyamSetu builds AI-assisted automations that connect your website, WhatsApp, lead forms, CRM-lite sheets, calendars, and reporting.

Hero copy: **Automate the repetitive work between a new enquiry and a converted customer.** From instant replies to lead routing and follow-up reminders, we help your team respond faster without hiring a full operations desk.

CTA: **Show us one repetitive workflow. We will suggest the automation.**

### 2. Pain section

The strongest local pain points are not abstract. A clinic misses calls during peak hours. A real estate team receives fifty WhatsApp messages but cannot identify serious buyers. A B2B supplier near Okhla gets quote requests without quantity, product code, or delivery information. A coaching institute runs ads but manually copies leads into a sheet.

The page should state the obvious: **manual follow-up breaks when lead volume grows.** Automation gives the owner a consistent process without making the business feel robotic.

### 3. What can be automated

Recommended blocks:

- Website form to WhatsApp/CRM routing.
- Instant WhatsApp acknowledgement and qualification questions.
- Lead scoring by service, budget, urgency, location, and source.
- Appointment or site-visit reminders.
- Missed-call or after-hours enquiry capture.
- Google Sheet or CRM-lite pipeline updates.
- Weekly lead reports sent in a simple WhatsApp-friendly format.
- FAQ reply drafts for price range, availability, documents, or next steps.

CTA: **Get a workflow map for your business.**

### 4. UdyamSetu automation stack

Frame the stack as simple and practical: landing pages, WhatsApp flows, tracking pixels, lead sheets, CRM-lite pipelines, message templates, and AI reply drafts. The page should avoid pretending every client needs enterprise software. Many South Delhi businesses need a reliable first layer: capture, qualify, route, remind, and report.

### 5. Implementation process

1. **Workflow intake:** capture current channels, lead types, staff handoffs, and common questions.
2. **Automation design:** decide what should happen instantly, what needs AI help, and what requires human approval.
3. **Message and form setup:** create qualification questions, reply templates, and escalation rules.
4. **Tool integration:** connect WhatsApp links, website forms, Sheets/CRM, calendar, and ad platforms where relevant.
5. **Testing:** run sample leads from Saket, GK, Hauz Khas, Okhla, or other target localities.
6. **Reporting loop:** track response time, qualified leads, missed leads, and next actions.

CTA: **WhatsApp 'AUTOMATE' for a workflow audit.**

### 6. Trust/proof section copy

Use clear promises: no black-box automation, no risky customer commitments, no fake lead guarantees, and no spammy bulk messaging. Show sample templates, screenshots of a CRM-lite board, lead status labels, and reporting snapshots. Add a proof block: **Every automation should reduce confusion for your team, not create another tool they ignore.**

### 7. Final CTA

End with a focused diagnostic: **Tell us the task your team repeats every day. We will tell you whether it should be automated with rules, AI, a better landing page, or a simple SOP.** Button: **Get My Automation Plan on WhatsApp.**

## FAQ section

### What business tasks can AI automation handle first?
Start with lead acknowledgement, qualification, routing, appointment reminders, CRM updates, reporting, and repetitive FAQ reply drafts.

### Can this work without a full CRM?
Yes. Many businesses can begin with a CRM-lite Google Sheet or simple pipeline, then upgrade once the workflow is proven.

### Will automation make customer replies feel robotic?
Not if the flow is written carefully. Use friendly acknowledgement, concise questions, and human handoff for pricing, sensitive issues, or complex queries.

### Is WhatsApp automation suitable for South Delhi service businesses?
Yes. It is especially useful where enquiries arrive while owners or staff are busy, such as clinics, real estate teams, local services, institutes, and B2B suppliers.

### How do we measure success?
Track response time, lead completion rate, qualified enquiries, source, follow-up status, appointment or visit bookings, and conversions.
