## 15. FAQ

**H2:** Straight Answers

**What is n8n, and why not just use Zapier?**
n8n does what Zapier does — connects your apps and automates the steps between them. But you can host it yourself, there's no charge per task, and you can drop in real code or AI when a workflow gets tricky. For most growing businesses it's cheaper and far more flexible.

**Do you offer managed n8n hosting?**
Yes. We set up n8n on a server we watch — backups, updates, alerts, the lot. You get the low cost of self-hosting without having to run anything yourself.

**Can you deploy n8n on my own server or with Docker?**
Yes. We do Docker-based deployments on your own server, a VPS, or providers like DigitalOcean. You keep full ownership. We keep it running.

**Is self-hosted n8n cheaper than n8n Cloud?**
Usually, yes. n8n itself is free. You pay for a server (often a few dollars a month) plus our setup and care. For most businesses that beats n8n Cloud's per-execution pricing — and your data stays on your own machine.

**My setup is broken and nobody maintains it. Can you take over?**
That's one of the most common things we do. We look at what's there, write down how it works, fix what's broken, and put it under proper maintenance.

**Is it safe to leave AI automation running on its own?**
It is when it's built right. We give the AI a clear job, check its output against real data, get a human to approve anything risky, and set up alerts so you know the moment something's off.

**Is my business data safe with self-hosted n8n?**
Self-hosting actually gives you more control, because nothing sits on someone else's servers. We handle logins, access, and backups as part of every setup.

**Do you work with businesses outside India?**
Yes. We work with clients anywhere. Everything is done remotely, and you own all the workflows and the setup.
